Meet CareBand

CareBand is a next‑generation wearable built for real‑time care, safety, and wellness. This prototype focuses on the experience and interface, ready to connect to real hardware later.

Designed with premium materials, intelligent sensors, and an AI engine that understands you. CareBand wraps care around your wrist, turning everyday moments into meaningful insights.
